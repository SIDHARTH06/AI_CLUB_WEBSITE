Auto contouring of HaN organs(Research Project) 
                                                                -Goutham P, Singam Sai Bala Subrahmanyam 

Contouring of the organs at risk(OAR) and target volume is a vital part of routine radiation therapy planning. At present radiation oncologist is required to manually outline(contour) the target volume to be irradicated and organs of risk to be spared, because of possible side effects. This is a very time-consuming process and also suffers from intra- and inter- observer variations. We introduce a fully automated deep learning model for segmentation of head and neck CTscans. It is built upon standard U-Net architecture. We use self-supervision techniques to boost the performance of the model by training using unlabeled images.
